Our Sponsors

Mountain Warehouse

Mountain Warehouse is a shop that specialises in outdoor supplies and clothes. They are giving a 20% discount on 26-29 April in their main shop located opposite the Church's entrance, which can be used by showing them this image.
.

Mary Janes

Mary Janes is a fish and chips takeway and restaurant found opposite the Co-op. They have a varied menu including gluten-free and vegetarian options. For the St George's Day Parade they are offering 10% off for anyone who shows them this page with the logo and discount.

Trespass

Trespass, located on the eastern end of Cromer's high street, sell outdoor clothing, as well as other adventuring equipment. They have been kind enough to provide a 20% discount on the day of the Parade, that can be used simply by asking.
.

Cromer Pier

Cromer Pier is a notable landmark, famous for its Cromer Pier Show. On the day of the Parade, they are offering £1 off a Whippy ice cream at either the Tidal Treats shop on the front of the pier or the Pavilion Theatre's bar. This offer is available to any group that includes someone in Scouting uniform.

Jarrolds

Jarrolds is a book shop that can be found towards the eastern side of the high street. They also sell craft materials and some toys. You can get 10% off if you spend £30 or more in their Cromer branch on the day of the Parade.
.

Blue Sky Cafe

Blue Sky Cafe is located on the Runton Road Car Park, where the parade starts. It's an ideal place for refreshments before the parade. Additionally, they are willing to provide a discount to anyone in Scouting uniform.
.
