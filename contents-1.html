Arrival And Parking

Drop Off

Assemble at west end of Runton Road Car Park, Cromer, NR27 9AU for 10:00. Once young people have been dropped off, you will need to use the Pay and Display car parks within Cromer (Runton Road, NR27 9AU; Meadow Road, NR27 9EE; Cadogan Road, NR27 9HT).

St George's Day Collection

This is in aid of About With Friends, a local charity that supports people with learning disabilities. It is thanks to the tremendous support of local communities such as ours that their work is possible. Please bring your Group's collection bags, which will be taken from each Group as they enter the Church.

Refreshments and Toilets

Toilets will be available prior to the Parade at the top of Runton Road Car Park. Blue Sky café at the entrance to the car park should be open for refreshments beforehand and during the day - please make sure they know that you are with the scouting community as they can then apply a small discount for any purchases.
