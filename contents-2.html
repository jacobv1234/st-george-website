Parade Route

The Parade

The Parade will set off at 10.30am.
The Parade will form-up and march in rows of three. Groups are asked to walk / march smartly at a reasonable pace, keeping up with the group in front.
Each Group is to be responsible for their own First Aid.

Accessibility

The Parade route is considered to be accessible for people in wheelchairs with a Carer, but anyone who feels the Parade route is too long can join their group as they arrive at the Church. Parents and carers may line the route on the pavement where there should be space for everyone to enjoy a good view of the Parade.

Flag Bearers

Flags should be held with holster on left shoulder, with flag pole in right hand, thumb uppermost near the nose and elbow level with shoulder. As each Group approaches the Church, their Flag Bearers will be directed to peel off to line the entrance of the Church to then fall in behind the end of the Parade.
